## Mission Overview

- **Your Goal:** To configure a simple AI assistant that helps you reply to emails faster and better by understanding your personal style.
    
- **The Process:** We will create a basic "briefing" document that defines your communication style and use it to configure a specialist assistant.
    
- **The Outcome:** You will have a hands-on understanding of how to apply context and test your creation with a simple, effective prompt.
    

---

## Step 1: Create Your "How I Write" Briefing

- Open a new text document. This will be the "brain" for your assistant. Answer these simple points:
    
- **My Role:** Briefly describe your professional role (e.g., "I am the Head of Marketing at a tech startup.").
    
- **My Tone & Style:** Describe your writing style (e.g., "My tone is professional yet friendly and encouraging. I keep paragraphs short and use bullet points for clarity.").
    
- **My Rules:** List 2-3 non-negotiable rules for your emails (e.g., "1. Always start by addressing the person by their first name. 2. Never end an email with 'Cheers'. 3. Always check for typos.").
    
- **Good Example:** Copy and paste the full text of a recent email you sent that you consider a perfect example of your style.
    

---

## Step 2: Configure Your Assistant

Now, let's bring your assistant to life in your preferred tool:

### For ChatGPT Users (creating a GPT):

- Go to the "Explore GPTs" section and click "Create".
    
- In the "Instructions" field, copy and paste the entire content of your "How I Write" briefing document.
    
- Give your GPT a name (e.g., "Email Response Assistant") and save it.
    

### For Claude Users (creating a Project):

- Start a new "Project" and give it a name (e.g., "Email Helper").
    
- Upload your saved "How I Write" briefing document directly into the project. The AI will now use it as context for all following prompts in this project.
    

---

## Step 3: Test Your Assistant

- It's time to see your work in action. Give your new assistant a task:
    
    - **The Context:** Find a real email you need to reply to.
        
    - **The Prompt:** In the chat with your new assistant, paste the email you received and add a simple instruction on top, like:
        
- "Using the context you have about my writing style, please draft a positive reply to this email confirming that I will attend the meeting."
    
- Review the draft. Does it match your tone and follow your rules?
