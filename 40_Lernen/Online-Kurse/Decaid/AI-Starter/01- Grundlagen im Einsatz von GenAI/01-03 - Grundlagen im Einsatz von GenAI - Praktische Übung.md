### Reflection: Your Workspace in the Light of GenAI

---

#### Adopt an AI-First Mindset

- The most important change starts with a new habit: a constant, critical look at your own workflow.    
- Before you start any task, stop and ask the central question: **"Can my AI assistant make me faster, better, or more creative here?"**    
- This isn't about replacing you; it's about giving you leverage to focus on work that truly requires your unique human skills.
    

### 03.1 Arbeitsalltag Analysebogen

#### Your Task: A 1-Week Workflow Analysis

- Your practical exercise is to systematically document your work week.    
- Identify and list your biggest **time sinks & routine tasks**. Where are your processes repetitive?    
- Note every instance of **creativity & research needs**. Where do you search for ideas or information from scratch?    
- Track all tasks involving **text generation & analysis**. Where are you writing, summarizing, or analyzing any form of text?

#### The Framework: How to Evaluate and Prioritize

- Now, use this simple framework to score the tasks you've documented and find the best starting point.    
- **Standardization Level:** How repetitive and rule-based is the task? (High score = perfect for AI).    
- **Data Basis:** Is there clear data or context available as input for the AI? (Clear input = perfect for AI).    
- **Leverage:** How big is the potential time saving or quality improvement? (High score = high priority).
